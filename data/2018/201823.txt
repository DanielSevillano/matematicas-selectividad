$$A = \begin{pmatrix}
    1 & 1 & 1 \\
    0 & 1 & 0 \\
    0 & 0 & 1
\end{pmatrix}, \quad
B = \begin{pmatrix}
    0 \\
    1 \\
    -1
\end{pmatrix} \quad \text{y} \quad
C = \begin{pmatrix}
    1 & 1 & 2
\end{pmatrix}.$$
