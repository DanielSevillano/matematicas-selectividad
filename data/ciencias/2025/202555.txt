$$r \equiv \frac{x+1}{4} = \frac{y+2}{3} = \frac{z-2}{-1} \quad \text{y} \quad s \equiv \begin{cases}
    x = 1 - \lambda, \\
    y = 2 + \lambda, \\
    z = -3 - 2\lambda.
\end{cases}$$
