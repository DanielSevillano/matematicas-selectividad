$$f(x) = \begin{cases}
    4x^2 + 16x + 17, & \text{si } x < -1, \\
    \dfrac{1}{3}(10-5x), & \text{si } -1 \leq x \leq 2, \\
    \frac{3}{2}, & \text{si } x > 2.
\end{cases}$$
